Frequently Asked Questions

Is this platform safe?

Yes. Security is our highest priority. We employ end-to-end encryption for all data traffic, secure authentication protocols, and run our infrastructure in a hardened cloud environment. Crucially, we are a non-custodial platform: you connect your own wallet, and your earnings are sent directly to you. We never take possession of your crypto assets.

What wallets are supported?

Our platform is designed for maximum compatibility. You can direct your mining rewards to virtually any standard Bitcoin or cryptocurrency wallet. This includes Hardware Wallets (Ledger, Trezor), Software Wallets (Exodus, MetaMask), and Exchange Wallets (Coinbase, Binance).

How does the mining software work?

We provide a managed mining service, handling all complex configurations. We utilize a highly optimized version of the BFGMiner engine to perform the computational work. You simply connect your wallet and monitor your progress through our web-based dashboard.

Is the free demo really free?

Absolutely. Our free demo is designed to give you a genuine experience of our platform's interface and performance. There is no credit card required and no obligation. It runs for a limited time with a capped hashrate so you can see firsthand how easy it is to get started.

What is BFGMiner?

BFGMiner is a world-renowned, open-source mining software celebrated for its performance and stability. We use BFGMiner as the core engine for our operations, adding a seamless user interface and managed infrastructure on top of this powerful core.
